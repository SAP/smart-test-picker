## Maintainer responsibilities
- Review and merge pull requests
- Triage and respond to issues
- Coordinate releases
- Manage repository access and administrative tasks

## Becoming a maintainer
New maintainers are added by consensus of existing maintainers, typically after sustained contribution to the project.
